What is The Bowling Lab

Fast bowling is a specialist skill. The best fast bowlers understand their action, know how to read batters, and head into every spell with a clear plan.

The Bowling Lab delivers expert coaching in real time during your club's indoor pre-season sessions. A different voice for your bowling group, with specialist focus on the skills that actually win games.
