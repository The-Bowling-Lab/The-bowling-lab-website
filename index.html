What is The Bowling Lab

A specialist voice for your bowling group.

Fast bowling is a specialist skill. The best fast bowlers understand their action, know how to read batters, and head into every spell with a clear plan.

The Bowling Lab slots into your existing indoor pre-season sessions and works with your fast bowlers in real time. No extra night to schedule. No disruption to your program. Coaches receive a written summary after every session to keep reinforcing the work through pre-season.

The idea came out of coaching at Wonga Park. I always wanted to bring someone in specifically for the fast bowlers — a different voice, someone who might put things across in a way that actually lands with the group. I couldn't find it, so I built it.

What's included

01

Technical assessment

Individual analysis of each bowler's action with practical feedback they can apply straight away.

02

Bowling plans

How to set up a batter, build pressure, and create wicket-taking opportunities during a spell.

03

Reading batters

Adjusting plans mid-spell based on what the batter is showing you.

04

Game awareness

Building pressure, setting fields, owning a spell. The mental side of fast bowling.

05

Individual feedback

Every bowler leaves with specific things to work on, tailored to their level and style.

06

Written summary for coaches

A full written summary after every session. Travel included.
